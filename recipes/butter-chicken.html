Butter Chicken

Velvety Butter Chicken Bliss

Indulge in Butter Chicken Extravaganza: Creamy, Dreamy, and Oh-So-Buttery Goodness!

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala, paprika)
  • Tomato puree
  • Heavy cream
  • Honey/sugar (optional)
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Let chicken get jiggy with yogurt and spices in a marination dance.
  2. Saut√© onions, garlic, and spice buddies in buttery bliss.
  3. Add tomato puree and groove until it's smooth and saucy.
  4. Reunite chicken with its tomato twirl partner in the pan.
  5. Pour a creamy cream chorus into the mix for extra flair.
  6. Adjust seasoning and sweeten the deal with honey, if you like.
  7. Simmer and shimmy until chicken is tender and flavors groove together.
  8. Garnish with coriander confetti and crunchy nut applause.
