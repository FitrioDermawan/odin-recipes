Szechuan Chicken

Szechuan Chicken Wows with Hot 'n' Spicy Shenanigans

Tender chicken pieces are wok-tossed to perfection with a medley of fiery spices, including Szechuan peppercorns, garlic, and chili paste.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken
  • Szechuan peppercorns
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Chili paste
  • Vegetable oil
  • Red bell pepper
  • Green onions
  • Sesame oil (optional for garnish)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with soy sauce, ginger, and garlic.
  2. In a hot pan, stir-fry the marinated chicken until cooked and set it aside.
  3. In the same pan, saut√© Szechuan peppercorns and chili paste for a spicy kick.
  4. Add sliced red bell pepper and green onions, and stir-fry for a couple of minutes.
  5. Toss the cooked chicken back into the pan and mix with hoisin sauce.
  6. Garnish with a drizzle of sesame oil (optional).
  7. Serve hot and enjoy the tongue-tingling flavors of Szechuan Chicken!
